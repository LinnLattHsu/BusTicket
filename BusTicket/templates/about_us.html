About MyBus Ticket

Your trusted partner for effortless bus travel across Myanmar. We're dedicated to simplifying your journey, one ticket at a time.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, reliable, and user-friendly bus ticket booking experience for everyone traveling within Myanmar, connecting travelers with a wide network of bus operators.

Our Commitment

We are committed to offering the most competitive fares and ensuring a reliable seat for all our passengers. Please note that once a ticket is booked, it cannot be canceled.

Easy Booking

Our intuitive interface allows you to book your bus tickets in just a few clicks. It's fast, secure, and hassle-free.

Wide Network

We partner with numerous bus operators to bring you a comprehensive selection of routes across Myanmar.

24/7 Convenience

Book your tickets anytime, anywhere, whether you're at home or on the go, with our round-the-clock service.
